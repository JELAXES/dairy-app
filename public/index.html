Our Roots

The Hearts Behind the Heritage

"Purely traditional, modernly delivered." — The Visionaries

Praveen Kusuma & Rashmi Praveen

Founded in 2024, Kshiralaya was born from Praveen and Rashmi's desire to reconnect families with the source of their nutrition. Driven by a passion for animal welfare and uncompromising purity, they've created a sanctuary where heritage dairy traditions meet modern excellence.

"Our farm is more than just a dairy — it's a commitment to the health of our community and the happiness of our herd."
